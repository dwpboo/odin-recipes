DB's Easy Carbonarra

Easy Italian Vibes

You'll never order in again with these 10 minute bangers

Ingredients

  • Spagetti
  • Guianchale, or Lardons (no need to be fancy)
  • Parmasan
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Get your European Noodles on
  2. Start frying off your meat, not in a sizzle, but bring it up to heat with the meat
  3. Whisk eggs and grate cheese into them and mix it all up
  4. Once noods are done, lob them in with the meat and coat them all up
  5. Pan off the heat, add the cheesy, eggy mix and stir the whole thing up
