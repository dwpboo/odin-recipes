DB's Quick peanut noods

Quick and easy noodles for a midweek flavour fix?

Who doesn't like peanutty, creamy noodles? Not me, that's for sure. With this recipe i can get my noodle fix lickety split.

Ingredients

  • Noodles. I like Udon for this, Soba work well too
  • Peanut Butter
  • Soy Sauce
  • Rice Vinegar, and some other bits
  • Whatever you like to chuck into a noodle dish

Steps

  1. Mix all the liquids up
  2. Cook noodles
  3. Prep whatever it is you plan on throwing into this
  4. Once noods are cooked throw it all together
