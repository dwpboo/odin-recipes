DB's Spatchcock Chicken

What's the point of spatchcocking a chicken?

I bloody love roasting up a chicken and having it ready to go for all sorts of random meals throughout the week. But with a couple of kids running around I don't have the time to bung it in the oven for the best part of two hours.

Ingredients

  • Whole Chicken
  • Sharp ass knife
  • Olive oil
  • Seasoning, I personally go Salt and Pepper so it's a base for loads of meals

Steps

  1. pre-heat oven to 220c
  2. Get rid of the backbone of the chicken
  3. Flatten that bird out and be sure to break the wishbone
  4. Get that bird in a tray and oil it up and season
  5. get it in the oven and dont touch it for 45 mins!
  6. leave it to rest a little, and dont forget to use those jammy juices from the pan
